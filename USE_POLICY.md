# Use Policy

This skill may be used for non-commercial, lawful, ethical research and education with attribution.

Do not use it to:

- Fabricate citations, data, experiments, peer-review outcomes, or literature coverage.
- Claim full-text review of paywalled or inaccessible papers that were not actually available.
- Bypass journal, institutional, copyright, privacy, or authorship rules.
- Submit AI-generated manuscripts without the human review, verification, and authorship accountability required by the target venue.
- Remove uncertainty or evidence boundaries from scientific claims.

Users are responsible for verifying references, permissions, journal formatting, figure rights, and final scientific accuracy.
